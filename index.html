Measure definitions
Manual
Skills to operate work processes without digital support — manually controlled equipment or non-IT-supported tools. Typical of work before the Digital Revolution (1st & 2nd Industrial Revolutions).
Digital
Skills to use digital/IT tools that support or indirectly manage the work process — e.g. CRM software, ERP systems, CNC machines. Computerisation wave from the 1970s onward (3rd Industrial Revolution).
Frontier
Skills to use the most advanced technologies — e.g. machine learning, AI systems, collaborative robots, digital twins. Fourth Industrial Revolution since the late 2000s.
